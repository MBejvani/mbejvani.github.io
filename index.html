Hi, I am Mohammad.
I am a Geophysicist.

I grew up in Karaj and received my M.Sc degree in Geophysics from the University of Tehran in 2014. During the M.Sc program, my research was focused on spectral decomposition. I was working under the supervision of Dr. Ali Gholami and Dr. Hamidreza Siahkoohi at the Institute of Geophysics. My scientific journey began with my undergraduate in physics, leading to my Master's research in the invasion and exploration of geophysics within a computational seismology group.
Interests:

  • Signal Processing
  • Seismic Imaging
  • Inverse problems and Regularization
  • Numerical analysis and Simulation
  • AI and Machine Learning
  • Near-Surface Geophysics
